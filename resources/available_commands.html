General commands /start - display the greeting /help - display this help /abort - stop any ongoing operation, i.e. preamble customization Subscribe control /Subscribe - subscribe your id to Doomsday related news /Unsubscribe - unsubscribe option Options /status - check online status of our servers in the most important cities and centers on the planet.
